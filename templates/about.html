About

Welcome to the Todo App!

This simple yet powerful app helps you organize your tasks and manage your time effectively. Whether you're tracking daily chores, work assignments, or long-term goals, the Todo App provides an easy-to-use interface to keep you on top of your to-do list.

Create, edit, and delete tasks effortlessly.

Add detailed descriptions and track when tasks were created.

Keep track of completed and pending tasks to stay productive.
This app is designed to help you focus, prioritize, and get things done. Whether you're using it for personal or professional purposes, the Todo App can help you stay organized and efficient.
